Question -1 : What is DOM and what is the purpose of DOM ?

Answer : DOM is the agent who shows you the way how to make relations between HTML and JS. You can do many more problem solving with JS, but as a developer if you don't know about DOM, you are nothing

Purpose of DOM -

1 . To make an interactive website.

2 . To make an Dynamic website.

3 . To make a perfect use.

4 . To make an updatable website.
